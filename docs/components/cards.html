Cards

Flexible and extensible content containers with multiple variants and options.

Basic Card

Cards are built with as little markup and styles as possible:

Card Title

Some quick example text to build on the card title and make up the bulk of the card's content.

Go somewhere 
<div class="card">
  <h3 class="fs-1 mb-3">Card Title</h3>
  <p class="mb-3">Some quick example text...</p>
  <a href="#" class="btn btn-primary">Go somewhere</a>
</div>

Card with Icon

    <svg class="gi gi-flash-outline ...">...</svg>
  </span>

  <p>This card includes an icon...</p>
</div>

Card Customization

Cards can be customized with utility classes:

  • .mb-* - Add margin bottom spacing
  • .p-* - Adjust padding
  • .text-center - Center align content
  • .h-100 - Make card fill height
